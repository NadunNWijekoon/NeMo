# **App Name**: NeMo

## Core Features:

- AI-Assisted Media Upload & Processing: Allow users to upload video and audio files. The system performs initial AI analysis such as auto scene detection for videos and transcribes audio into text (via Whisper tool).
- Basic Timeline Editor: A streamlined web-based timeline interface for arranging uploaded media, performing fundamental cutting/trimming, and real-time previewing of edits.
- AI Editing Assistant: Utilize an AI tool to interpret natural language prompts (e.g., 'Make this cinematic', 'Add suspenseful background music') to apply suggested edits or asset selections directly within the editor.
- AI Background Removal & Audio Enhancement: Provide basic AI-powered tools for background removal in video clips and intelligent noise reduction or voice enhancement for audio tracks.
- Auto Subtitle Generation & Editing: Automatically generate subtitles from video audio using a speech-to-text tool, allowing users to review and make minor edits to the generated text.
- Project Management & Cloud Saving: Enable users to save their editing projects, view a dashboard of recent projects, and ensure cloud synchronization across sessions (utilizing Firebase/Supabase for storage and database).
- Basic Export & Share: Allow users to export their finished projects to common formats like MP4, with options for quality, and provide basic sharing functionalities.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A clear and modern blue (`#478AF3`), evoking intelligence and fluidity, chosen to stand out subtly against light backgrounds.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated blue-gray (`#EBF0F6`), serving as a soft, minimal canvas that aligns with a 'liquid glass' aesthetic.
- Accent color: A vibrant cyan (`#24B0C7`), approximately 30 degrees from the primary on the color wheel, adding a contrasting pop for interactive elements and subtle gradients.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif), for a modern, techy, and futuristic feel that complements the 'liquid glass' UI. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), for clean readability and neutrality in larger text blocks and interface labels.
- Sleek, outlined icons for interactive elements, focusing on clarity and functionality within the 'liquid glass' aesthetic to enhance the modern and precise user experience.
- Modular layout structured with translucent, layered cards that feature soft shadows and subtle gradient overlays, creating a sense of depth and a 'frosted glass' effect. Key interactions will be accessible via prominent floating action buttons.
- Subtle, fluid micro-interactions and transitions, such as delicate hover effects, smooth card expansions, and effortless timeline scrolling, to enhance the 'liquid' feel and user engagement throughout the application.
